Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the recovery process typically take?

The recovery process varies depending on the complexity of your case. Simple cases may take 2-4 weeks, while complex investigations can take 3-6 months. We keep you updated throughout the process.

Do you charge upfront fees?

No. We operate on a contingency basis, meaning we only collect fees when we successfully recover your funds. There are no upfront costs.

Is my information kept confidential?

Yes, absolutely. All your information is encrypted and protected under attorney-client privilege. We never share your details with third parties without your consent.

What if I lost my funds on an exchange that shut down?

Even if the exchange no longer operates, we have methods to trace and recover your funds. Contact us with your case details, and we'll evaluate your options.

Can you help if the scammer is international?

Yes, we work with international law enforcement agencies and have partnerships worldwide. We can pursue cases involving international scammers.

How do you trace cryptocurrency transactions?

Our blockchain experts use advanced forensic tools to trace transactions through the blockchain. Even though crypto is pseudonymous, the transactions are traceable and permanent on the ledger.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does account verification take?

Standard verification typically takes 24-48 hours. Expedited verification is available for premium members.

What are the minimum deposit and withdrawal amounts?

Minimum deposit is $100 for standard accounts. Minimum withdrawal is $50. Some payment methods may have different limits.

How long do withdrawals take?

Withdrawal processing typically takes 1-3 business days depending on your payment method. Bank transfers may take 3-5 business days.

Is my account insured?

Yes, we maintain segregated client accounts and carry insurance coverage up to $500,000 per client. Details available on request.

What trading hours are available?

We offer 24-hour trading, 5 days a week. Markets are open from Sunday 5 PM EST to Friday 5 PM EST.

How can I reset my password?

Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive an email with reset instructions within minutes.

Complaint & Dispute Resolution

If you have a complaint or dispute, we're committed to resolving it fairly and quickly:

  1. Contact our support team: Describe your complaint in detail
  2. Investigation: We'll investigate your complaint within 5 business days
  3. Resolution: We'll provide a written response with our findings
  4. Appeal: If unsatisfied, escalate to our compliance department
  5. Arbitration: If still unresolved, we'll offer independent arbitration

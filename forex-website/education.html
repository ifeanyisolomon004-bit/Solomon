Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to learn forex trading?

It depends on your dedication. Basics can be learned in 2-4 weeks, but mastery takes months of practice and experience with real market conditions.

Do I need to be good at math?

Basic math skills are helpful but not essential. Our platform handles all calculations. Focus on understanding concepts and developing good trading discipline.

Can I make money with small deposits?

Yes, leverage allows you to trade large positions with small deposits. However, leverage also increases risk, so proper risk management is crucial.

Is forex trading suitable for beginners?

Yes, but start small and with a demo account. Use our comprehensive education resources to build knowledge before risking real money.

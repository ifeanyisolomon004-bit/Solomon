Popular Trading Strategies

Scalping

Make quick trades to profit from small price movements. Requires high volume and tight stops.

  • Duration: Seconds to minutes
  • Risk: High
  • Skill Level: Advanced

Day Trading

Open and close positions within a single trading day. Good for active traders.

  • Duration: Minutes to hours
  • Risk: Medium
  • Skill Level: Intermediate

Swing Trading

Hold positions for several days to capitalize on market swings and trends.

  • Duration: Days to weeks
  • Risk: Medium
  • Skill Level: Intermediate

Position Trading

Long-term strategy focusing on major market trends. Less time-intensive.

  • Duration: Weeks to months
  • Risk: Variable
  • Skill Level: Beginner to Intermediate

Order Types & Execution

Market Order

Execute immediately at the current market price. Used for instant entry into a position.

Limit Order

Execute only when the price reaches your specified level. Better control over entry price.

Stop Order

Activate at a trigger price for risk management. Commonly used for stop losses.

If-Touched (One Cancels Other)

Two related orders where execution of one automatically cancels the other.

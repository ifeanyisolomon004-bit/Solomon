Admissions

Begin Your Journey at Solomon Academy

Admission Process

1

Application

Submit the application form along with required documents including school records and birth certificate.

2

Entrance Test

Applicants take an entrance assessment to evaluate their academic readiness and aptitude.

3

Interview

Parents and students are invited for an interview to discuss educational goals and school values.

4

Admission Decision

Results are communicated within 2 weeks of the interview. Confirmation of admission completes the process.

Admission Requirements

Primary School (Grade 1-6)

  • Birth certificate (original)
  • Previous school records
  • Medical certificate
  • Passport-size photographs (4)
  • Age: Minimum 5 years for Grade 1

Secondary School (Grade 7-10)

  • Birth certificate (copy)
  • Previous school records (2 years)
  • Character certificate
  • Medical report
  • Entrance exam performance

Senior Secondary (Grade 11-12)

  • Academic transcripts
  • Character certificate
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Entrance assessment score
  • Stream preference (Science/Commerce/Humanities)

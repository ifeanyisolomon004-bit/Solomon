About Solomon Academy

Our Story, Mission, and Values

Our Mission

Solomon Academy is committed to providing a world-class education that empowers students to become critical thinkers, responsible citizens, and leaders of tomorrow. We believe in fostering intellectual curiosity, creativity, and character development in a supportive and inclusive environment.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and the holistic development of students who positively impact society and contribute to global progress.

Our History

2001

Founded

Solomon Academy was established with a vision to provide quality education to underprivileged communities.

2008

Expansion

Expanded to include a secondary school section and built new state-of-the-art facilities.

2015

International Recognition

Received international accreditation and joined the global school network.

2023

Digital Transformation

Implemented comprehensive digital learning platform and modern technology in all classrooms.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in everything we do.

Integrity

We uphold honesty and strong ethical principles.

Inclusion

We celebrate diversity and ensure every student feels valued.

Innovation

We embrace creative thinking and new approaches to education.

Community

We foster strong relationships and collaborative learning.

Responsibility

We develop socially conscious and responsible citizens.

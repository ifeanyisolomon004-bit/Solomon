Frequently Asked Questions

What is the admission process?

The admission process involves application submission, entrance assessment, interview, and final admission decision. Detailed information is available on our Admissions page.

What are the school timings?

School operates from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM on weekdays. Extended care facilities are available until 5:00 PM for primary students.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer merit-based and need-based scholarships to deserving students. Contact our admissions office for details.

What transportation facilities are available?

We provide school buses covering various routes. A monthly transportation fee is charged. Details available at the office.

Can I visit the campus?

Yes, campus tours are organized for prospective parents and students. Please schedule in advance by contacting the admissions office.

What extracurricular activities are offered?

We offer sports, arts, music, drama, science clubs, and various other activities to develop well-rounded individuals.
